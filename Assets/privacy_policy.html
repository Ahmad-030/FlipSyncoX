Overview

Welcome to FlipSyncoX, a memory card matching game developed by Arsalan Limited Production. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of our application.

FlipSyncoX is a fully offline game. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All game data resides exclusively and securely on your own device.

By using FlipSyncoX, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the application.

// 02

Information We Collect

FlipSyncoX stores only the minimum data necessary to deliver core gameplay functionality — and it never leaves your device.

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Best scores per difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert)
  • Best completion time and move count per level
  • In-progress game state (card positions, matched pairs, timer, moves)
  • Selected difficulty preference

Data we do NOT collect — ever:

  • Name, email, phone number, or any contact information
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IP addresses
  • Location, GPS, or region data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral tracking
  • Camera, microphone, contacts, or file system access
// 03

How We Use Data

All locally stored game data is used exclusively to enhance your gameplay experience:

  • Displaying your personal records on the High Score screen
  • Restoring an in-progress game via the Continue feature
  • Remembering your preferred difficulty level between sessions

This data is never transmitted to us or any third party. It exists only within your device's sandboxed storage and is readable only by the FlipSyncoX application.

// 04

Storage & Retention

All data is stored using Flutter SharedPreferences — a secure, OS-managed local storage mechanism. This data:

  • Never leaves your device and is never uploaded anywhere
  • Is automatically erased when you uninstall the application
  • Can be manually cleared via your device's App Info settings at any time
  • Is not actively backed up by FlipSyncoX (your device's own backup policy may apply)
We retain no copies of your data. Once removed from your device, it is permanently and irrecoverably deleted — we cannot restore it.
// 05

Third-Party Services

FlipSyncoX does not integrate any of the following:

  • Analytics platforms (Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, Meta Audience Network, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs or login systems
  • Crash reporting or monitoring services
  • Any network-connected API or external service

The app is distributed via the Google Play Store. Google's distribution platform has its own privacy policy, which you can review at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

FlipSyncoX is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. We are committed to compliance with applicable children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR-K.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No account registration or personal data entry is required to play
  • The game contains no in-app purchases, advertisements, or external links directed at children
  • All content is age-appropriate and free of harmful material

If you believe a child has inadvertently provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will take prompt action.

// 07

Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally and we never receive or process your personal information, your primary control rests directly with your device. You have the right to:

  • Access — All stored data is managed on-device via Android App Info
  • Delete — Uninstall the app or use "Clear Data" in device settings
  • Opt Out — Simply uninstall FlipSyncoX at any time, with no consequences

EU/EEA residents have additional rights under GDPR. Since we do not process personal data, most GDPR provisions do not apply in practice. Nevertheless, you may contact us with any privacy concern and we will respond promptly.

// 08

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect application integrity and your data:

  • FlipSyncoX operates fully offline — no internet permissions are requested or used
  • The app runs within Android's secure sandboxed environment
  • No sensitive personal data is ever requested, stored, or processed
  • Application binaries are signed and distributed through official channels only

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to our contact email below before public disclosure.
